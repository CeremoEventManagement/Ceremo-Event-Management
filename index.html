What Is AppStorm?

Ceremo is an all-in-one event management solution designed to help event planners, organizers, and hosts create, manage, and promote events. The app provides a comprehensive set of features that make event planning and management a breeze, from ticketing and registration to promotion and marketing, attendee management, analytics and reporting, and more.

With Ceremo, event planners can easily create and manage events, customize event details such as name, date, time, location, and description, and sell tickets and allow attendees to register for events with secure payment processing and customizable registration forms. The app also includes promotion and marketing features such as social media integration, email invitations, and customizable landing pages to help event organizers reach a wider audience and boost attendance.

Ceremo's advanced attendee management features allow organizers to keep track of attendees and their information with features like check-ins, guest lists, and attendee feedback. The app also provides valuable insights into event performance with analytics and reporting features that track attendance, revenue, demographics, and more.

In addition, Ceremo offers an easy-to-use in-app messaging system to keep organizers, attendees, speakers, and vendors connected, as well as push notifications to send reminders and updates to attendees before and during events. The app can also be customized with your own branding and color scheme to create a seamless experience for your attendees, and can be synced with popular calendar apps to help attendees stay organized and never miss an event.

Key Features

Ceremo is an all-in-one event management app that allows users to easily create, manage and monitor events. Its key features include mobile app integration, attendee management, vendor and artist connections, and real-time tracking. With Ceremo, users can save time and ensure seamless event execution.

  • Invitation System

    The app allows users to create events and invite guests, artists, and friends to attend. This feature provides a hassle-free way to manage guest lists and send out invitations.

  • Location-Based Services

    The app uses the user's location to provide them with sound managers, DJs, or any other required service providers within a 100km radius. This feature ensures that users can easily find and book the services they need for their event.

  • Vendor Connect

    The app connects event organizers with vendors, such as decorators and venue providers, so they can easily communicate and book services without wasting time on multiple phone calls or emails.
